Hybrid Power & Biofuel 101

The Innovation Race: From V8 to Hybrid Efficiency

Formula 1's adoption of the complex Hybrid Power Unit (V6 Turbo + ERS) has positioned the sport as a key driver for automotive energy innovation. The technology, focusing on energy recovery (ERS-K and ERS-H), maximizes efficiency and reduces fuel consumption, directly contributing to sustainability goals.

Sustainable Fuel: Biofuel 12 & Beyond

F1 is committed to achieving Net-Zero Carbon by 2030, with a major focus on developing 100% sustainable fuels. Biofuel 12 is a key milestone, dramatically reducing the sport's carbon footprint while maintaining performance levels. This technological push benefits not only the sport but also the wider automotive and transport sectors.
